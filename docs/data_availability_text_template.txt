This study releases an open repository containing: (1) the sample-level metadata table for 202 HPLC samples, including timestamps and geolocations; (2) the manifest of processed PACE/OCI hyperspectral image scenes used during benchmark construction; (3) the per-sample HPLC-to-PACE mapping table; (4) the scripts used to construct the PACE-HPLC benchmark indices; and (5) a semi-theoretical synthetic identifiability dataset with reconstruction scripts for methodological stress testing of gate-controlled selective interpretation. The repository is available at https://github.com/xigua-pi/PACE-HPLC .

The released materials are intended to support full traceability of the reported benchmark and reproducibility of the methodological stress-test analyses. Some internally generated AC intermediate products, internal HPLC raw tables, and laboratory absorption datasets are not distributed in this repository because they are subject to internal workflow constraints or third-party data-sharing boundaries. However, the published benchmark indices, scene identifiers, sample identifiers, timestamps, geolocations, synthetic benchmark files, and reconstruction scripts are provided to allow readers to trace each reported matchup and reproduce the released simulation-based analyses.
